KPMG is embedding an AI system into the software 276,000 employees use for client work

By Plainly Staff  ·  May 26, 2026  ·  7 min read

The accounting giant is wiring Anthropic's Claude directly into the platform that handles its global tax work. All 276,000 employees in 138 countries will get access. This is bigger than another corporate AI announcement — KPMG is not handing employees a chatbot to play with on the side.

It is rebuilding the platform that handles client work around an AI — and raising real questions about accuracy, accountability, and who actually does the math.
